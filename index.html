Fly Eagles Fly!

The Philadelphia Eagles are a professional American football team based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The team was founded in 1933, competing in the National Football League (NFL) as a member of the NFL's NFC East Division. The Philadelphia Eagles are known for their passionate fan base and their iconic midnight green and kelly green uniforms. The Eagles have appeared in five Super Bowls, winning two of them.
